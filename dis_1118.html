\(X\sim \text{G}(p)\) \(f(x)=p(1-p)^{x}\) \(\text{E}(X)=\frac{1-p}{p}\) ,x:
\(X\sim \text{Poisson}(\lambda)\) \(f(x)=\frac{\rm e^{-\lambda}\lambda^{x}}{x!}\) \(\text{E}(X)=\lambda\)
\(X\sim \text{Unif}(a,b)\) \(f(x)=\frac{1}{b-a}\) \(\text{E}(X)=\frac{a+b}{2}\)
\(X\sim \text{Exp}(\lambda)\) \(f(x)=\lambda\rm e^{-\lambda x}\) \(\text{E}(X)=\frac{1}{\lambda}\) :
\(X\sim \text{N}(\mu,\sigma^2)\) \(f(x)=\frac{1}{\sqrt{2\pi\sigma^2}}\rm e ^{-\frac{(x-\mu)^2}{2\sigma^2}}\) \(\text{E}(X)=\mu\)
